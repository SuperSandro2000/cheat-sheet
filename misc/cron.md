## Misc

MOST IMPORTANT LINES IN A CRONTAB! Allow to run normal programms that are not under /bin/

```shell
SHELL=/bin/bash
PATH=/usr/local/sbin:/usr/local/bin:/sbin:/bin:/usr/sbin:/usr/bin
```

or 

```shell
SHELL=/bin/bash
BASH_ENV=/home/hotpi/.bash_profile
```
